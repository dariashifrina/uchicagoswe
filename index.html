What We Do

Academics & Career

In true UChicago fashion, we believe in constantly expanding our knowledge. From fireside chats with university professors to formal lecture series, SWE facilitates learning and discussion across a wide variety of fields.

Service & Outreach

We believe in engaging the public and connecting meaningfully with young women in the Chicagoland area; we demonstrate this by encouraging and supporting youth to explore science and engineering by hosting and participating in various events and gatherings.

Professional Development

SWE has a wide network of mentors and industry professionals willing to help you build and navigate your future, whether that be grad school, a career, or something else entirely.

Community

Mutual Support and Solidarity are essential elements of SWE; we enjoy building and maintaining strong relationships with each other, creating a welcoming and inclusive community.
