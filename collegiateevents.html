Hyde Park Engineers Panel

We organizing a panel of female and non-binary undergraduate students to discuss their experiences in different STEM majors at UChicago. Are you interested in STEM and are considering studying some field within it after high school? Concerned about applications? Looking to find a community and have a conversation about the practical steps of university life as a STEM student as well as mental health and creating impact? If so, please join us on November 23rd, from 6-7:30pm CST. We'd love to have you join our conversation.
